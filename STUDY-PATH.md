# STUDY-PATH: Start Here

This guide helps you choose an efficient path through the SP Study Guide based on your role and timeline.

## Module dependency graph (text view)

```text
01 Foundations
   ↓
02 IGP at Scale → 03 BGP at SP Scale → 04 MPLS Core
                                                ↓
05 Traffic Engineering → 06 Segment Routing
                                                ↓
07 L3VPN Services → 08 L2VPN/EVPN
                                                ↓
09 Transport/Optics → 10 Slicing/5G
                                                ↓
11 Automation/Telemetry → 12 Case Studies
```

## 12-week study schedule

| Week | Focus | Modules/Sections | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Fundamentals | Module 01 + Module 02.1–2.2 | Solid SP routing underlay baseline |
| 2 | IGP operations | Module 02.3–2.4 | Convergence and protocol-selection confidence |
| 3 | BGP core | Module 03.1–3.2 | Scalable iBGP design patterns |
| 4 | BGP edge policy | Module 03.3–3.4 | Transit/customer policy and TE control |
| 5 | MPLS foundations | Module 04 | Label-plane operations and troubleshooting |
| 6 | TE models | Module 05 | RSVP-TE vs SR-TE design tradeoffs |
| 7 | Segment Routing | Module 06 | SR-MPLS/SRv6 architecture and migration |
| 8 | L3VPN services | Module 07 | PE/VRF/MP-BGP service delivery |
| 9 | L2VPN and EVPN | Module 08 | EVPN control-plane and multihoming ops |
| 10 | Optical transport | Module 09 | Packet-optical integration decisions |
| 11 | Slicing and 5G | Module 10 | SLA-driven transport slicing planning |
| 12 | Automation + integration | Modules 11–12 | Telemetry, CI/CD, and end-to-end design synthesis |
